Managing rental properties doesn't have to be complicated. Whether you've just purchased your first apartment or you're moving away from spreadsheets, Vermietler makes it easy to get organized.

## Step 1: Add your first property

After signing up, the onboarding wizard guides you through creating your first property. Enter the address, property type, and year built. That's it — you can always add more details later.

## Step 2: Create your units

Each property can have multiple units. Add apartments, commercial spaces, garages, or parking spots. For each unit, specify the size, number of rooms, and current status (occupied, vacant, or under maintenance).

## Step 3: Add tenants and leases

Link tenants to their units by creating a lease. Enter the rent amount, deposit, payment cadence, and lease dates. Vermietler will track everything from here.

## Step 4: Connect your bank

This is where the magic happens. Connect your bank account and Vermietler will automatically import transactions and match them to tenant rent payments. No more manual checking.

## What's next?

Once your property is set up, explore these features:

- **Document vault** — Upload leases, invoices, and receipts
- **Meter readings** — Track water, heating, and electricity consumption
- **Nebenkosten statements** — Generate operating cost statements with our guided wizard

Welcome to easier property management.
